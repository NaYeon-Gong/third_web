About Time

About Time is a 2013 British romantic comedy-drama film written and directed by Richard Curtis, and starring Domhnall Gleeson, Rachel McAdams and Bill Nighy. The film is about a young man with the ability to time travel who tries to change his past in hopes of improving his future.

The film was released in the United Kingdom on 4 September 2013. The film received mixed reviews from critics. The review aggregation website Rotten Tomatoes gives the film a rating of 69%, based on 154 reviews, with an average rating of 7.86/10. At the box office, it grossed $87.1 million against a $12 million budget.
